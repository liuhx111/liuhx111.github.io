Hangxin Liu

I am a Master's student in Mechanical Engineering at UCLA. Currently I work at the Center for Vision, Cognition, Learning, and Autonomy with Professor Song-Chun Zhu. My research interests lie on Computer Vision and Robotics.

Before joining VCLA, I received a B.S. in Mechanical Engineering and a B.S. in Computer Science with a Mathematics minor from Virginia Polytechnic Institute and State University (Virginia Tech) in 2016. At Virginia Tech, I was a research assistant in Computational Multiphysics Systems (CMS) Laboratory with Professor Tomonari Furukawa. My works at CMS were supported by Murata Electronics North America, Inc and NSF-EAGER-1554961
