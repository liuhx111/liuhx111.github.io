Hangxin Liu

I am a Ph.D student in Computer Science at UCLA, working in the Center for Vision, Cognition, Learning, and Autonomy with Professor Song-Chun Zhu. I also recevied a M.S. in Mechanical Engineering from UCLA. My research interests lie on Robotics and Computer Vision, and I am supported by DARPA XAI grant N66001-17-2-4029.

Before joining VCLA, I graduated with a B.S. in Mechanical Engineering and a B.S. in Computer Science with a Mathematics minor from Virginia Polytechnic Institute and State University (Virginia Tech) in 2016. At Virginia Tech, I was a research assistant in Computational Multiphysics Systems (CMS) Laboratory with Professor Tomonari Furukawa. My works at CMS were supported by Murata Electronics North America, Inc and NSF-EAGER-1554961
